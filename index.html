Myda foods has been established to cater for the breakfast needs of childern of all ages, including adults. Khadija Erasung,
founder of Mydafoods struggled with meals that agrees with her first child nutritional needs. Upon several attempts to get the child
to eat right, proved abortive, she started coming up with innovative ways to boost her infant baby's intake of especially nutritious but healhty breakfast.
The founding of Mydafoods stemmed from this simple but summarized mother's quest to feed her child right, culminating in this awesome first batch
of cereal products for healthy breakfast for children as well as adults as Supermix, Morill Cart, and b-Soya!
Recently Mydafoods unveiled its fresh spices, dubbed MydaTouch, like everything Mydafoods unveils it is already getting thumbs-up by all and sundry!!!
